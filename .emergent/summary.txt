<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to build a private, invitation-only application initially called Gathering Cypher, later renamed to **Kindred,** with the tagline Where your circles gather and grow. The app is designed as a digital home for communities like families, churches, and fraternities to plan events, store memories, and manage community affairs without ads or data mining.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS (MVP & BEYOND):**
-   **Events Hub:** Create private, recurring events with roles, RSVP tracking, agenda, volunteer/potluck sign-ups, and Zoom link attachments for hybrid events.
-   **Memory Vault:** A private media archive for photos and files with commenting. Future plans include voice notes and AI tagging.
-   **Legacy Threads:** A feature for building family trees, story threads, and preserving oral history.
-   **Shared Contributions:** A system for collecting dues, fees, and offerings using integrations like Stripe and RevenueCat.
-   **Community Decisions:** Tools for polls and voting.
-   **Authentication & Profiles:** Standard email/password and Google Sign-In, password recovery, and detailed user profiles with account deletion.
-   **Communication:** Announcements and an internal chat feature for each gathering.
-   **Onboarding:** A polished flow for new users to create their first community (Courtyard).
-   **Notifications:** A comprehensive system including reminders, unread badges, history, and user preferences (browser and push).
-   **Monetization:** Tiered subscriptions (Stripe) and add-on purchases, with mobile app store integration (RevenueCat).
-   **Mobile & Desktop:** Full PWA support and a native mobile app wrapper (Capacitor).
-   **Legal:** Privacy Policy and Terms of Service pages accessible within the app.
